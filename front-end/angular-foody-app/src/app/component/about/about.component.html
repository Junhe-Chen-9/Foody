The reason we building this application

At the begening, I thought this is my personal problem, that I would spend minuets tring to figure out where to eat, and end up not going or choose a crappy restraunt. Sometimes me and my friends would sit in the cars and no one can give a good sugesstion on which restraunt to go for dinner. It has become an siginificant issue, somtimes choosing can be hard to accomandates everyone in the group. So I build this application that choose the restraunt for you. Our goal is to learn from random choosed restraunt and based on your input and learning to choose the next restrauntfor you, and when there are multiple users we will choose the restraunt that everyone likes based on your vote on previous restraunt experiences.
